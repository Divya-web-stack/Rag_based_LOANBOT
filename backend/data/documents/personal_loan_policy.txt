Personal Loan Policy - 2024 Edition
----------------------------------
- Eligible for salaried and self-employed individuals aged 21 to 58.
- Minimum monthly income: ₹25,000 (salaried), ₹40,000 (self-employed).
- Tenure: 1 to 5 years.
- Interest Rate: 10.5% - 14% p.a.
- Documentation: PAN, Aadhaar, last 3 months' salary slips or ITR.
- Prepayment allowed after 6 months.
